## Overview

**Productive Panda** is a beautifully designed productivity app built with Flutter that helps you manage your time, crush your goals, and build a daily focus habit. Whether you're a student, developer, or anyone trying to be more intentional with their time — Productive Panda has your back.

---

## Features

### Pomodoro Timer
- Elegant, animated countdown timer with smooth transitions and a polished UI
- Customizable work and break intervals
- Visual and haptic feedback at session end

### Goal Management
- Create goals with a **title**, **description**, and **deadline**
- Break each goal down into **tasks** — a dedicated to-do list attached to every goal
- Set individual deadlines per task to stay on track
- Mark tasks complete and watch your goals come to life

### Home Screen Scoreboard
- At-a-glance stats right when you open the app:
  - **Time Worked** — total focused time logged
  - **Goals Completed** — milestones you've hit
  - **Days Streak** — consecutive days you've shown up

## Design Philosophy

Productive Panda is built around the idea that a productivity tool should itself be a *pleasure* to use. Every animation, color choice, and interaction is crafted to feel calm and encouraging — not stressful or cluttered. The panda mascot embodies that energy: focused, steady, and a little delightful.
